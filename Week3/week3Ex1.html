The United Kingdom, made up of England, Scotland, Wales and Northern Ireland, is an island nation in northwestern Europe. England – birthplace of Shakespeare and The Beatles – is home to the capital, London, a globally influential centre of finance and culture. England is also site of Neolithic Stonehenge, Bath’s Roman spa and centuries-old universities at Oxford and Cambridge. ― Google Capital: London Dialing code: +44 Population: 67.22 million (2020) World Bank Prime minister: Boris Johnson Minimum wage: 1,598.69 EUR per month (Jun 2020) Eurostat
